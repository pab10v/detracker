Filtering & Privacy Policy

1. Quality Standards

DeTracker follows a strict set of principles to ensure that privacy protection does not come at the cost of a broken web. Performance is our first priority.

2. What We Block

DeTracker is designed to intercept Intrusive Trackers, Fingerprinting attempts, Beacons, and Anomalous URL Parameters.

3. What We Do NOT Block

We prioritize essential functionality (login systems, CAPTCHAs) and contextual search ads if enabled by the user.

4. Transparency

Every block action is recorded in the local Forensic Log. Users can inspect the zScore and the Signals for every intercepted request.
